# Source Registry (Future Reference)

This document catalogs **potential future sources** of public assistance information.
It is **not used by v1 logic** and does not imply integration or endorsement.

Help Nearby links users to official resources but does not host, verify, or guarantee aid.

---

## Federal & National Resources (Official)

- DisasterAssistance.gov — Federal disaster aid and recovery programs
- Ready.gov — Disaster preparedness and safety guidance
- FEMA — Emergency management and recovery resources
- FEMA App — Alerts, preparedness, and shelter info
- Small Business Administration (SBA) — Disaster loans
- USDA Disaster Resource Center — Agricultural and rural disaster support
- National Child Traumatic Stress Network — Child & family recovery resources
- ADA National Network — Disability-specific disaster guidance
